Welcome to Paradise

Your Caribbean Dream Awaits

Nestled on the pristine shores of world-renowned Seven Mile Beach, The London House offers an unparalleled luxury escape in the heart of the Cayman Islands. Our beautifully appointed condominiums combine Caribbean charm with modern sophistication.

Following major renovations in 2025, each unit features modern updates with elegant interiors, central air-conditioning, ceiling fans, and private patios or balconies overlooking the spectacular blue Caribbean Sea.
